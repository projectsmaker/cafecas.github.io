What is Video Poker?

 

Chances are youve played poker at some point. Texas Holdem is the game of choice these days, but when poker was first making its way across the United States, Five-Card Draw was the game they played. Its still a familiar home game, and arguably the simplest way to play; you receive five cards, then you get one chance to throw away as many cards as you like and draw new ones. Once the draw is finished, you have your final five-card poker hand.

 

In live poker, the general object is to have either the highest-ranking hand at the end, or to get all of your opponents to fold. But there arent any other opponents when you play video poker online for money its just you and the machine. Your payouts depend solely on the strength of the hand you make, using the following standard poker hand rankings as a baseline:

Two Pair (Kh-Kd-Qc-Qh-4s)

Pair (Ac-As-8d-4h-3c)

 

In order to win a prize at video poker, you need to have a hand thats strong enough to qualify. Jacks or Better is the simplest game on the menu; if you have a pair of Jacks (or Kings, Queens or Aces), youll get paid out at even-money for each coin you bet. Your hand could fall under any of several dozen categories, which the strategy will list from best to worst; choose the highest category on the list that describes your hand, and keep those cards. For example, lets say you get dealt the following cards in Jacks or Better:

 

Ks-Qs-Jc-4c-3d

 

You dont have a made hand (i.e. a winning hand) yet, but you do have three useful cards: the King, Queen and Jack. You could keep those and draw to a straight.
